About Me


I am currently an undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science and Mathematics. I have a passion for technology, math, design, and helping others. As a Girls Who Code Alumni, I have gained the tools and skills to move forward with my career goals. Moreover, I have taken the initiative to bring Girls Who Code College Loops to Eastern CT State in hopes of encouraging women in STEM! Did you know? In this 21st century, only 24% of computer scientists are female. In addition to the gender gap in the computer science field, there also exists a racial inequality in the tech idustry.
